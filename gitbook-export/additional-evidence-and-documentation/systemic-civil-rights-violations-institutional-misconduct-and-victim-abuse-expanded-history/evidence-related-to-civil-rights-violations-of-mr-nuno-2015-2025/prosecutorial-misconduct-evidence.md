# Prosecutorial misconduct evidence

This page maps Brady/suppression themes and a malicious prosecution checklist to record types.

* Back to [Evidence of Civil Rights Violations, Misconduct, YWCA RICO Predicates, et al](./)

### Brady / suppression themes

Common suppressed or minimized items include:

* Compliance and monitoring data.
* Weak/absent results from broad digital searches.
* Conflict indicators and credibility problems within complainant narratives.

### Malicious prosecution checklist (mapped to evidence)

1. **Charges filed**.
2. **Lack of probable cause** (or evidence inconsistent with PC).
3. **Favorable termination** (dismissal with prejudice).
4. **Improper motive** (retaliation and coordinated pressure).
5. **Damages** (see damages section).
