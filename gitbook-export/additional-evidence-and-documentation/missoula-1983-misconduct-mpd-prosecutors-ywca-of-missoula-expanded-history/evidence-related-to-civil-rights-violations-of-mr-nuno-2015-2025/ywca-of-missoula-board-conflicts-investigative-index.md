### FAQ

#### What is the core conflict of interest issue?

It is the risk created when law enforcement and prosecutorial actors have governance roles inside a victim-advocacy organization.

This includes board-level integration signals and downstream case impacts.

#### Why does a board conflict matter for client safety?

Victim-services organizations must be able to act independently.

Perceived or actual conflicts can chill reporting and increase revictimization risk.

#### What is the Nuno case relevance?

The Nuno case is treated as the primary evidence spine.

It provides a timestamped pattern to test governance conflict → operational harm.
