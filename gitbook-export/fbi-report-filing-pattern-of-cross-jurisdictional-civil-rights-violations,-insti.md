## Summary

This statement documents over a decade of systematic constitutional violations, institutional corruption, and coordinated harassment perpetrated against Elvis Ryland Nuno by law enforcement agencies, prosecutorial offices, and the YWCA Missoula across Washington State and Montana.

> DoJ Civil Rights Division received this matter on August 31, 2025 (Report #658793-SKB) and referred it to the FBI for investigation of criminal civil rights violations.
