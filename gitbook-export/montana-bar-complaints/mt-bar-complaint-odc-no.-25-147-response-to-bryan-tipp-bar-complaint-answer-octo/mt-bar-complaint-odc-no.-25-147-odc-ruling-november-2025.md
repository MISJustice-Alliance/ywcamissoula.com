# MT Bar Complaint ODC No. 25-147:  ODC Ruling (November 2025)

{% include "../../.gitbook/includes/odc-25-147-related-misjustice-alliance-case-file.md" %}

## Office of Disciplinary Council’s Response to Case ODC 25-147

The ODC ultimately ignored the scope of the grievance as filed, ruling in Tipp's favor, agreeing with his mischaracterization of the complaint as a disagreement over the scope of what Mr Tipp was retained for, ultimately ruling that because the case was eventually dismissed that Mr Tipp provided adequate representation. A right to review was filed in further repeated attempts to correct the record as to what the original grievance concerns, and calls into question the narrow qualification that because of the case's eventual dismissal that Mr Tipp upheld his professional duties.
