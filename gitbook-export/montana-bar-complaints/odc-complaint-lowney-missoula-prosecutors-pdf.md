## Synopsis

This document is a formal grievance filed with the Montana Office of Disciplinary Counsel against Deputy County Attorney Brian Lowney and the Missoula County Attorney's Office, alleging prosecutorial misconduct during the prosecution of stalking charges against Elvis Ryland Nuno that were ultimately dismissed with prejudice. The grievance asserts violations of multiple Montana Rules of Professional Conduct, including suppression of exculpatory evidence, reliance on a biased investigator, and failure to disclose judicial rulings and conflicts of interest.
