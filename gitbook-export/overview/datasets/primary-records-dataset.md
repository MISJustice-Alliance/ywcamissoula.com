### Coverage

* **Temporal:** 2012–present (core evidentiary spine: 2015–2025)
* **Geographic:** Missoula, Montana; Seattle and Edmonds, Washington

### Provenance

Records are sourced from court filings, agency correspondence, published case exhibits, and other primary documentation.

### Limitations

* Some index entries may be placeholders pending a stable public link.
* Some documents may be redacted for safety, privacy, or legal reasons.
