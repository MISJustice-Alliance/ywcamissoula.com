# Dataset catalog

## Dataset catalog

Use these landing pages as the **canonical URL** for each major record collection. They’re designed for citation, reuse, and Google indexing.

### Dataset landing pages

* [Primary records dataset](primary-records-dataset.md)
  * Police reports, court filings, and correspondence.
  * Coverage: 2012–present (core spine: 2015–2025).
* [Official complaints dataset](official-complaints-dataset.md)
  * DOJ/FBI filings and state-level complaints with supporting packets.
  * Coverage: 2016–present (major packages: 2025).
* [Timeline & claims map dataset](timeline-and-claims-map-dataset.md)
  * Time-ordered events, relationship context, and claim framing.
  * Coverage: 2014–2025 (plus earlier supporting context).
