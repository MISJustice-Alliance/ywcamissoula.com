# YWCA Complaint & Google Reviews; Other Victims of YWCA Misconduct (2018-2020)

## Overview

This document compiles evidence from formal complaints and public reviews documenting patterns of misconduct and institutional failures at YWCA Missoula. The collected accounts demonstrate systemic issues rather than isolated incidents.

## Formal Complaints Filed

### [2017 Complaint to YWCA President](https://cr-2024-002-evidence-16_misjusticealliance.arweave.net/)

* Documented staff misconduct by E'Lise Chard and Rebecca Pettit
* Requested organizational accountability
* Accompanied by $400 goodwill donation
* No response or investigation conducted
* Coordinated retaliation immediately followed receipt of complaints
* Additional Google Review's were posted [\[1\]](https://cr-2025-002-evidence-19_misjusticealliance.arweave.net/) [\[2\]](https://cr-2025-002-evidence-7_misjusticealliance.arweave.net/), triggering escalating retaliation.

## Google Review Documentation

Public reviews from 2018-2020 reveal consistent patterns:

* Lack of responsiveness to community concerns
* Institutional protection of staff over accountability
* Failure to address documented misconduct

## Pattern Analysis

The documented complaints and reviews establish:

1. Systematic failure to investigate staff misconduct
2. Retaliation against those who file complaints
3. Institutional capture preventing accountability
4. Coordination between YWCA and law enforcement
