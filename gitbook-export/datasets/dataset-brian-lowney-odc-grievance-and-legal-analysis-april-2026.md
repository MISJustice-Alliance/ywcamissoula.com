The Lowney grievance advances allegations of prosecutorial misconduct that complement and extend the pattern documented in the ODC 25-147 complaint against Bryan Tipp. Key analytical significance:

* **Pattern reinforcement**: If the ODC acknowledges merit in the Lowney grievance, it corroborates the systemic-prosecutorial-misconduct theory advanced across CR-2025-001 through CR-2025-003.
* **Timeline continuity**: The April 2026 filing maintains the evidentiary chronology and demonstrates ongoing institutional accountability gaps.
