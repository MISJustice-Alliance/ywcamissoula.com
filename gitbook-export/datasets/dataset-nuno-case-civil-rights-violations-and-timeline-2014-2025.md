# Dataset: Nuno case civil-rights violations and timeline (2014–2025)

### Executive snapshot

This dataset is the canonical landing page for the Nuno case **timeline + record index** (2014–2025). It is intended to be a stable crawl target for search and citations and to route readers into the event-by-event timeline and primary record indexes.

### Scope

* **Timeframe:** 2014–2025
* **Geography:** Washington and Montana
* **Topics:** First, Fourth, and Fourteenth Amendment claims; §1983 theories; retaliation; unlawful search/seizure; malicious prosecution
* **Record types:** filings, orders, police reports, correspondence, published complaint packets

### What this dataset is (and isn’t)

This dataset is:

* a curated index of primary records and “entry point” packets
* a navigation hub into the event-by-event timeline and evidence maps

This dataset is not:

* a court finding or adjudication
* a substitute for reading the underlying filings and orders
