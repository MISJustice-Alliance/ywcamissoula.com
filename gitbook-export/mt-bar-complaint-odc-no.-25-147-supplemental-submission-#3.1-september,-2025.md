# MT Bar Complaint ODC No. 25-147: Supplemental Submission #3.1 (September, 2025)

This supplemental submission provides additional evidence and analysis regarding Attorney Bryan Tipp's professional conduct violations. This submission is part of the ongoing documentation supporting ODC No. 25-147.

## Summary

This addendum expands on previous submissions with additional documentation of:
- Failure to identify and challenge constitutional violations
- Inadequate discovery and evidence gathering
- Missed opportunities for dismissal motions
- Cumulative impact of professional negligence

## Professional Standards Analysis

Montana Rules of Professional Conduct require attorneys to provide competent representation including:
- Rule 1.1: Competence - legal knowledge, skill, thoroughness and preparation
- Rule 1.3: Diligence - reasonable diligence and promptness
- Rule 1.4: Communication - keeping client informed
- Rule 3.1: Meritorious Claims and Contentions
