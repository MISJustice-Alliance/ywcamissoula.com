# Motion to seal/redact Miranda evaluation (Nov 2025)

This motion was submitted to Edmonds Municipal Court on November 3, 2025, regarding a HIPAA violation committed by Dr. Marta J.L. Miranda, Psy.D. (Washington License PY60498191) in a court-ordered psychological evaluation.

## Key Issues:

* The evaluation contained numerous fabricated statements verifiable through audio recordings
* Misrepresented statements from family members who were never informed of the purpose of their interview
* Cognitive test results were selectively "thrown out" to further a contrived narrative
* Diagnosis failed to meet basic required criteria outlined in the DSM-IV

## Hearing Status:

This motion was scheduled for a hearing on December 2nd, 2025. During the hearing the Judge could not find the evaluation in the case files (despite it being filed twice). A continuance was called for to collect additional evidence and re-submit the motion for review.
