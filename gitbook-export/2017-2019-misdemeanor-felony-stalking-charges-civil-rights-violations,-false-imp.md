# 2017–2019 stalking charges (MT): civil-rights violations

## Introduction

This comprehensive legal analysis examines the systematic civil rights violations, false imprisonment, and prosecutorial misconduct of misdemeanor and felony stalking charges that resulted from Elvis Nuno's formal complaint to the YWCA of Missoula in 2017.

The case ultimately resulted in **dismissal with prejudice** in 2018, confirming what was evident from the beginning: no viable criminal case existed. The documented damages exceed **$3.44 million**.
