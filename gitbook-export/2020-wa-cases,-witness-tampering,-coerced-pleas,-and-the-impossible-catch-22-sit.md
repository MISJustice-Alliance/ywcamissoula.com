# 2020 WA cases: witness tampering and coerced pleas

## Overview

This document analyzes the 2020 Washington cases involving allegations of witness tampering, coerced pleas, and the systemic catch-22 situation created by the legal process.

## Witness Tampering Allegations

* Pattern of witness intimidation
* Coordination between accusers and law enforcement
* Evidence of communication to influence testimony

## Coerced Plea Analysis

* Pressure tactics employed
* Inadequate legal representation during plea process
* Involuntary nature of plea entry

## The Catch-22 Situation

The impossible situation where:

* Challenging the plea requires admitting guilt
* Maintaining innocence prevents access to certain remedies
* Systemic barriers to justice
