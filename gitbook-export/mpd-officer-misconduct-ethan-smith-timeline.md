# MPD Officer Misconduct Series: 2018 Complaint Timeline

**Officer:** Ethan Smith (Badge #345)  
**Complainant:** Tyrone Nuno (father of Elvis Ryland Nuno)  
**Recipient:** Sgt. Dustin Delridge, Missoula Police Department  
**Date:** March 2018  
**Location:** Missoula, Montana

## Summary

Beginning in June 2017, Officer Ethan Smith repeatedly contacted Tyrone and his ex-wife, Ellie Nuno, appearing at Ellie's residence and making multiple phone calls, messages, and letters about their son Elvis. Tyrone reports that, after one brief cooperative conversation, Smith continued to contact them despite their having no information, which he describes as "highly unprofessional and unethical conduct" and "incessant harassment" of uninvolved family members.

## Key Allegations

In the complaint, Tyrone states that Smith "has no reason to believe" Elvis is doing anything wrong and is "simply fishing for information" in a way that "embarrass[es], humiliate[s], and intimidate[s]" his son. He demands that all contact with him and his family "cease and desist immediately."

## Institutional Response

Archive summaries note that following this complaint, Smith was removed from any case involving Elvis Nuno and E'Lise Chard due to the appearance of impropriety toward the Nuno parents.
