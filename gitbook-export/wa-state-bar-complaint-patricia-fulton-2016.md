# WA State Bar Complaint: Patricia Fulton (2016)

{% file src=".gitbook/assets/cr-2025-002-complaint-14_misjusticealliance.arweave.net.pdf" %}

***

On December 15th, 2015, I retained Patricia Fulton as a defense attorney in a domestic violence related case in Edmonds.

## Key Issues:

1. **Order of Protection Mishandling**: Ms. Fulton advised not to attend the hearing, then revealed it was impossible to ever appeal the decision since no defense was entered.
2. **Inadequate Defense Preparation**: Ms. Fulton refused to interview family members or the complainant herself, and was dismissive of therapist input.
3. **Mental Health Evaluation Issues**: The only step in building defense was to receive a mental health evaluation from Associated Behavioral Health Center (ABHC), which refused to provide a full evaluation.
4. **Failure to Obtain Discovery**: Ms. Fulton failed to find and provide copies of several charges out of Seattle, which would have been absolutely vital to the defense.
5. **Follow-Through Failure**: Ms. Fulton clearly stated she would provide the court with a letter from ABHC but failed to do so, putting freedom in serious jeopardy.

## Conclusion:

Ms. Fulton has been completely negligent in upholding her responsibility to provide an adequate defense.
