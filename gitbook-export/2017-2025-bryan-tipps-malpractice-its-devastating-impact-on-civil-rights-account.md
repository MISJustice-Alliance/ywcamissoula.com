### Element-by-element malpractice assessment

1. **Duty** - Tipp owed fiduciary and professional duties under MRPC 1.1-1.4
2. **Breach** - Failed to advise of limitations periods, negligent case assessment, refusal to act
3. **Causation** - But-for cause of every SOL lapse
4. **Damages** - $3.44 million documented losses; $6.4-8.4 million potential recovery lost

### Damages model (assumes liability)

### Risks, defenses, and uncertainties (non-exhaustive)

* **Accrual and tolling**: discovery rule, continuing violation, and factual notice timing can change deadlines.
* **Causation**: requires showing the underlying claims were likely to succeed.
* **Damages proof**: requires a defensible “but-for” earnings and loss model.
* **Immunities**: many underlying §1983 theories face qualified or absolute immunity issues.

These are case-specific.

They need primary authority review.

### Litigation outlook (non-advice)

* **Timeliness**: Filing before 2028 satisfies Montana's discovery rule
* **Measure of Damages**: Value of lost underlying claims plus consequential loss
* **Proof Strategy**: Expert testimony establishing standard-of-care breaches
