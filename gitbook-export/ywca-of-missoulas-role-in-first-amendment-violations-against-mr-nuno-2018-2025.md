## I. Institutional Framework and Legal Foundation

### A. YWCA's Quasi-Governmental Function and First Amendment Obligations

The YWCA of Missoula operates as a nonprofit organization providing government-funded services and maintaining close institutional relationships with law enforcement and prosecutorial offices. Under the state action doctrine, private entities can be subject to First Amendment constraints when they exercise powers traditionally performed by government.

## II. The 2017 Complaint: Protected First Amendment Activity

Mr. Nuno's formal 2017 complaint to the YWCA President constituted core petition activity under the First Amendment's Petition Clause. The complaint:

* Detailed systematic false police reports by E'Lise Chard and Rebecca Pettit
* Documented abuse of organizational positions to facilitate harassment
* Requested cessation of misconduct and organizational accountability
* Included a goodwill donation demonstrating support for the YWCA mission
