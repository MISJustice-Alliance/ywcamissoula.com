## Summary

I respectfully submit this complaint regarding a pattern of civil rights violations, conflicts of interest, and abuse of power by Missoula Police Department personnel - specifically Ethan Smith and Connie Brueckner - and by MPD as an institution, connected to matters involving the YWCA of Missoula and culminating in an unlawful arrest and false imprisonment in or around August 2018.

### Civil Rights Violations

* **Unlawful Seizure and False Arrest**: Arrested without probable cause and detained contrary to the Fourth and Fourteenth Amendments
* **Due Process Violations**: Material exculpatory information was disregarded or not properly documented/disclosed
* **Retaliatory/Malicious Enforcement**: Enforcement actions appear motivated by bias or animus
* **Equal Protection Concerns**: Handling of case deviated from standard MPD practices

### Conflicts of Interest and Coordination Concerns

* **Improper Coordination with YWCA of Missoula**: MPD personnel relied heavily on YWCA-affiliated narratives without adequate independent investigation
* **Role Blurring and Undue Influence**: Communications indicate blurred lines between advocacy and police functions
* **Failure to Recuse/Disclose**: Known relationships presenting conflicts were not disclosed

### Abuse of Power and Evidence/Records Irregularities

* **Mishandling of Case Materials**: MPD failed to secure, preserve, or accurately log key records and evidence
* **Suppression/Selective Use of Evidence**: Exculpatory leads were ignored while inculpatory narratives were emphasized

### August 2018 Incident: Lost in Missoula County Jail

* **Unlawful Arrest and False Imprisonment**: Taken into custody without valid basis and subsequently lost within the jail system
* This occurred while already on GPS monitoring in full compliance

## Requested Remedies

1. **Independent Investigation**: Full, impartial investigation by the Montana Department of Justice
2. **Records Audit and Preservation**: Immediate audit of MPD and jail records
3. **Findings and Accountability**: Formal findings on constitutional violations
4. **Corrective Policy Measures**: Clear separation protocols for interactions between MPD and advocacy organizations
