# WA State Dept. of Health Complaint: Dr. Marta Miranda (2016)

Dr. Marta Miranda is a "forensic psychologist." She was, at the time, the only doctor who had the required credentials to complete a court-ordered psychological evaluation as part of satisfying the terms of a plea deal regarding a 3rd Degree DV Assault charge.

This was the first case in the long series of similar criminal charges that would follow over the years, and most notably, at the age of 31, it was the first "serious" criminal charge or conviction in Mr Nuno's life, up to that point.

## Key Issues:

* The evaluation contained numerous fabricated statements verifiable through audio recordings of the sessions
* Misrepresented statements from family members who were never informed of the intent or purpose of their interview
* Cognitive test results were selectively "thrown out" to further a contrived narrative
* Diagnosis failed to meet basic required criteria outlined in the DSM-IV

## Subsequent Actions:

A complaint filed with the Washington Department of Health ([2017-3122PY](https://cr-2025-002-evidence-8_misjusticealliance.arweave.net/)) by Mr. Nuno, his family, and his therapist of four years was denied without explanation of how the case was investigated.
