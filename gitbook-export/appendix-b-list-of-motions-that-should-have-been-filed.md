# Appendix B: List of Motions That Should Have Been Filed

### A. Constitutional Challenge Motions
1. Motion to Dismiss for First Amendment Violation (Protected Speech)
2. Motion to Dismiss for Selective Prosecution (Fourteenth Amendment)
3. Motion to Suppress Evidence (Fourth Amendment)
4. Motion to Dismiss for Due Process Violations

### B. Discovery Motions
1. Motion to Compel Discovery of Detective Brueckner's YWCA Board Records
2. Motion for Brady Material (exculpatory evidence)
3. Motion for Investigator Conflict of Interest Disclosure

## II. Trial Motions

1. Motion in Limine to Exclude Prejudicial Evidence
2. Motion for Directed Verdict
3. Motion to Dismiss for Insufficient Evidence
