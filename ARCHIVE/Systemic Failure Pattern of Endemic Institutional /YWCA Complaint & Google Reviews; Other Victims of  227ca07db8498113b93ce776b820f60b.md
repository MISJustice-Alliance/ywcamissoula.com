### Letter Text:

June 3, 2018

Deirdre Flaherty
YWCA of Missoula
1130 W Broadway St
Missoula, MT 59802
Dear YWCA President,
I am writing to officially notify your organization of my grievances towards particular actions and behavior committed by employees of the Missoula YWCA office, E’Lise Chard and Rebecca Pettit.
Motivated by vindictive intent and resentment following the termination of my romantic involvement with her sister after she became frighteningly abusive and violent, E’Lise Chard has established a pattern of filing false police reports against me, and encouraging others to harass me with threats of violence and death.
Recorded incidents began in 2016 in the state of Washington, and after I was forced to leave Washington in fear for my safety, resumed in Montana and continue through present day.
In June of 2017, E’Lise Chard and accomplice Rebecca Pettit, utilizing their positions within your
organization, and under the guise of official business, contacted Officer Ethan Smith (badge #345). They knowingly and repeatedly made false statements and on multiple occasions presented fabricated evidence, and attempted to use my career in IT as evidence against me, in an attempt to convince this officer I was responsible for an elaborate "cyber crime spree."
In direct response, Officer Smith has repeatedly made off-record, aggressive, and intimidating phone calls, incessantly harassed my parents (which in large part lead my family being forced to legally commit my mother for mental health evaluation and treatment), and attempted to charge me with five criminal citations.
Although this contact was for personal, and not professional, purposes the offcer informed me they were made through their place of employment. This harassment has been conducted through off-the-record abuse of your organization’s time and resources in order to present a facade of legitimacy. The situation has caused a considerable amount of distress for myself and my entire family, and foremost represent an outrageous abuse and misuse of professional positions and resources within your organization.
My family has filed a formal complaint against Offcer Ethan Smith regarding his nearly year long
aggressive harassment of myself and my family, and I have every intention of proceeding with civil action. 
I expect this behavior is not representative of the standard of ethics to which your organization adheres.
Please consider this letter a formal grievance. I request necessary protocol be taken within your organization to ensure these activities cease and desist immediately, and to hold the responsible parties accountable for their behavior.
I support the mission of your organization and appreciate the vital contribution to the community that it provides. As a gesture of good will, and to show my support for this vital service I am enclosing a donation to your organization. However, should these events persist, I will consider them to be sanctioned practice, and will be given no choice but to consider the YWCA of Missoula also accountable for the hardship endured by myself and my family. I trust that will not be necessary, but am prepared to protect myself in the event that it becomes so.
Thank you very much for your time and consideration.
Sincerely,
Elvis Nuno
