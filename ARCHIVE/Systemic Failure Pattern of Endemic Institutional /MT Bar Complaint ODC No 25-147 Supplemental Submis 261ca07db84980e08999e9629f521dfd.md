## I. SUMMARY: Supplemental Evidence of Legal Malpractice - Failure to Address Demonstrable Perjury and False Statements

This addendum provides critical evidence of attorney Bryan Tipp's professional negligence in failing to recognize, address, and counter demonstrably false statements and perjury contained in E'Lise Chard's June 12, 2018 Petition for Temporary Order of Protection. The attached document contains multiple fabricated statements that directly contradict established facts, court records, and E'Lise Chard's own sworn testimony, yet Mr. Tipp failed to develop any legal strategy to address this systematic manipulation of the legal system.

Mr. Tipp's failures constitute violations of Montana Rules of Professional Conduct 1.1 (Competence), 1.3 (Diligence), and 3.1 (Meritorious Claims and Contentions) through his inexcusable failure to counter demonstrable perjury that formed the basis for continued prosecution and civil rights violations against his client.

## II. DOCUMENTED FALSE STATEMENTS IN E'LISE CHARD'S PETITION

### A. **Fabricated Personal Relationship Claims**

**False Statement in Petition**: "I have known Elvis Nuno approximately 3 years. I did not know him prior to his relationship with my twin sister, Danielle Chard."

**Documented Reality**: E'Lise and Mr. Nuno first met in late 2001 during high school when she made a single hostile statement "I hope you know I'm obligated to hate anyone my sister dates." Their total interaction history consists of:

- 2001: Brief, hostile introduction
- 2015: Emergency communication seeking safe housing for her sister
- 2015: Several communications expressing concern for Danielle's mental health
- 2017: Three brief emails requesting cessation of harassment

**Evidence Available to Tipp**: Mr. Nuno provided detailed timeline documentation showing the minimal contact history, yet Tipp failed to use this to establish E'Lise's lack of personal knowledge and credibility.

### B. **Perjurious Claims Regarding Threats and Violence**

**False Statement in Petition**: "Throughout the past several years, Elvis has threatened to try to get my children removed from my care, verbally stated that 'when Danielle and I get back together, she'll never see you again' and that I am the 'most disgusting person on the face of the earth.'"

**Documented Reality**:

- Judge Warren specifically found Mr. Nuno's communications constituted protected First Amendment speech
- E'Lise admitted under oath that Mr. Nuno made no contact attempts beyond three emails
- No evidence of threats was ever produced despite extensive investigation
- All criminal charges were dismissed with prejudice

**Tipp's Negligence**: Despite having Judge Warren's finding that the conduct was protected speech, Tipp failed to use this judicial determination to establish E'Lise's perjury.

### C. **False Drug History Allegations**

**False Statement in Petition**: "Elvis is known by many police officers and courts to have abused drugs both in Washington and here in Missoula."

**Documented Reality**: No such drug history exists in any court records or police reports. E'Lise had no personal relationship that could provide her knowledge of any alleged drug use.

**Available Evidence**: Court records, criminal history reports, and medical records all contradicted these claims, yet Tipp failed to present contradictory evidence or pursue perjury charges.

### D. **Fabricated Employment Interference Claims**

**False Statement in Petition**: "Within an hour of his sentencing Thursday, Elvis contacted my employer, YWCA Missoula, pressing for me to be fired or in some way punished."

**Documented Reality**: The timing alleged is impossible as Mr. Nuno was in court proceedings and then traveled directly home. Phone records and witness testimony could have established his whereabouts, demonstrating the impossibility of E'Lise's claims.

**Tipp's Failure**: Despite having access to court schedules and the ability to subpoena phone records, Tipp made no effort to disprove this easily verifiable false statement.

## III. LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE OF FALSE STATEMENTS

### A. **Criminal Perjury Under Montana Law**

Under Montana Code Annotated § 45-7-201, E'Lise Chard's false statements under oath constitute criminal perjury:

- Made false statements under oath in judicial proceedings
- Statements were material to the proceedings
- Made with knowledge of their falsity
- Affected the court's decision-making process

### B. **Criminal Defamation Violations**

The false statements constitute criminal defamation under MCA § 45-8-212:

- Knowingly communicated defamatory matter to third parties (court, law enforcement)
- Exposed Mr. Nuno to hatred, contempt, and injury to his occupation
- Made without consent and with knowledge of falsity

### C. **Abuse of Legal Process**

The petition represents systematic abuse of the legal process through:

- Filing false sworn statements to obtain court orders
- Weaponizing protection order procedures for harassment purposes
- Leveraging YWCA position to provide false legitimacy to baseless claims
