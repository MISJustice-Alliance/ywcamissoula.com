# Summary of Protection Order Filing

E’Lise Chard’s narrative in her June 12, 2018 Petition for Temporary Order of Protection at every stage attempts to insert herself as a central figure—both as an alleged target of threats and as a supposed causal factor in the demise of the relationship between Mr Nuno and Danielle Chard. These claims collapse under basic scrutiny of both documented factual history and legal standards for witness foundation, relevance, and prejudice.

# Analysis of False Narrative

## 1. **Absence of Foundational Knowledge: Parties Are Strangers**

**Legal Principle:** Montana courts recognize that for testimony to be relevant and admissible, the declarant must have firsthand knowledge (Rule 602, Montana Rules of Evidence). Statements that constitute speculation, conjecture, or are plainly outside the declarant’s personal experience or observation are subject to exclusion.

**Facts:**

- All available evidence and documentation show minimal direct contact between E’Lise Chard and Mr Nuno: a hostile high school introduction in 2001, emergency outreach by Mr Nuno in 2015 to request housing help for Danielle after Mr Nuno had already ended the relationship due to abuse, and a handful of communications related strictly to Danielle’s well-being and mental health concerns stemming from ongoing communications displaying escalating erratic behavior.
- Both Danielle and E’Lise are estranged, with no history of mutual support, advice-giving, or significant familial interaction; E’Lise communicated to Mr Nuno that she had “disowned” Danielle when they were 18 (in 2001), as she described Danielle as a “pathological liar” and “abusive to men.” This “disownment” was also confirmed by Danielle and regularly a topic of discussion with Mr Nuno. Their estrangement extended to the point where E’Lise did not know which college degrees Danielle spent 6 years of graduate and post-graduate work to receive.

**Legal Consequence:** E’Lise’s claims about her involvement in the relationship, her knowledge of its inner workings, or any influence she exercised are fundamentally **inadmissible speculation**. She had neither proximity, nor practice, nor invitation to be privy to the couple’s private matters.

## 2. **No Causal Role in Relationship Termination—Direct Contradiction to Documentary Evidence**

**Facts:**

- Documentation, email, and contemporaneous witness statements all show that Danielle’s abusive behavior in 2015 led **Mr Nuno** to end the relationship, requiring her to leave his home causing Mr Nuno to fear for his safety, and despite the target of extreme abuse Mr Nuno seeking out E’Lise’s intervention solely as a matter of personal conscience to avoid forcing Danielle into a situation where she was left homeless without safe or stable housing. This further demonstrates Mr Nuno displayed no animus towards either Danielle or E’Lise, presented no threat, and went out of his way to see that Danielle did not come to any harm, in spite of her abuse.
- At no time did Mr Nuno attribute any responsibility for the relationship’s failure to E’Lise; no communication indicates any statement to that effect, nor did Danielle’s own claims in court filings ever include any mention of E’Lise.

**Legal Analysis:**

E’Lise cannot attribute to herself a motive or blame assigned by Mr Nuno that has no foundation in the words, conduct, or actions of the parties actually involved. Any assertion that Mr Nuno blamed or threatened her for “destruction of the relationship” not only fails any evidentiary standard (lacking even hearsay support), but is contradicted in records and actions of Mr Nuno.

## 3. **Estrangement and Complete Lack of Relevance or Insight**

**Facts:**

- E’Lise and Danielle’s estrangement predates all events relevant to the order. Their only contact was “short, superficial, and impersonal discussions,” making E’Lise “far outside the judgement” of either Danielle or Mr Nuno.
- E’Lise could not, from her position as an outsider, have observed or meaningfully opined on the circumstances at issue.

**Legal Principle:**

Montana law and the rules of evidence exclude characterizations, motives, or intent attributions made by those without meaningful, contemporaneous, firsthand knowledge of the persons or events described (see State v. Clark, 2002 MT 377). Testimony about the thoughts, intentions, or mental state of another party by a stranger is classic speculation.

## 4. **Impossibility of Insight into Decision-Making or Motivations**

E’Lise’s order-of-protection petition is replete with statements such as “Elvis blames me for …,” “Elvis believes his legal situation is my fault,” or “he believes I stand between them,” all of which are impossible for her to truthfully assert unless Mr Nuno made direct statements to her (which is disproven by the record).

**Legal Standard:**

Montana courts will reject testimony “based exclusively on an individual’s purported speculation or assumptions regarding another person’s beliefs, intention, or causation.”

# Conclusion of Facts

E’Lise Chard had no valid basis, as a legal or factual matter, to make claims about:

- The reasons behind the end of the relationship
- Any supposed blame, threat, or targeting by Mr Nuno
- Mr Nuno’s mental state, intentions, or future plans
- Any role she played in Mr Nuno’s decision to remove Danielle from his home

Given their mutual estrangement and E’Lise’s exclusion from any role in the relevant events, her narrative is not only **factually unsupported and misleading** but precisely the sort of inadmissible, prejudicial assertion that should have been objected to and precluded at every legal stage.

## **Key Consequence**

The reliance on E’Lise Chard’s narrative by law enforcement, prosecutors, or the court—without direct evidence or foundational support—serves as compelling evidence of a lack of due diligence, probable cause, and impartiality in all proceedings relying on her statements. This is further aggravated in the context of her YWCA role and her pattern of leveraging institutional authority to bolster inherently speculative or false claims.

# Analysis of Perjurious Statements

E'Lise Chard's order of protection application further contains numerous demonstrably false statements that constitute perjury under Montana law and represent a continuation of a decade-long harassment campaign that has resulted in over $3.4 million in documented damages to Mr. Nuno. The document fundamentally misrepresents the minimal contact history between parties who were essentially strangers and ignores judicial findings that Mr. Nuno's conduct constituted protected First Amendment speech.

## 1. **Fabricated Relationship History**

**E'Lise's Claim**: 

Portrays an extensive relationship with knowledge of personal details and criminal history.

**Documented Reality**: 

The complete contact history between Mr. Nuno and E'Lise Chard consists of only four minimal categories of interactions over 16 years:

- **Late 2001**: Brief hostile introduction where E'Lise stated "I hope you know I'm obligated to hate anyone my sister dates"
- **February 2015**: Emergency contact seeking safe housing for her sister Danielle after domestic violence forced Mr Nuno to remove Danielle from his home out of fear for his safety
- **2015**: Outreach expressing concern about Danielle's deteriorating mental health resulting from calls received from Danielle consisting of unprovoked anger and rage of indeterminate reason, cause, and incredibly concerning as they lacked any general coherence.
- **2017**: Three brief emails requesting cessation of harassment

**Legal Significance**: 

This minimal contact history establishes that E'Lise had no personal knowledge to support her detailed accusations, making her statements inherently false and defamatory.

## 2. **False Claims About Criminal History**

**E'Lise's Claim**: 

Alleges Mr. Nuno has "a couple of domestic violence charges" and extensive drug history known to "all the police and judges."

**Documented Reality**:

- Only **one** DV 3rd degree assault charge in Washington State, which Mr. Nuno was coerced into pleading to and spent substantial resources attempting to withdraw
- **No drug history, criminal or personal** - this claim is entirely fabricated
- Seattle harassment and cyber stalking case dismissed with prejudice due to lack of evidence, with subpoenaed phone records showing mutual communication as late as October of 2015
- Montana cases dismissed with prejudice after 18 months

**Legal Significance**: 

These false statements about criminal history constitute criminal defamation under Montana law MCA 45-8-212.

## 3. **Perjurious Statements About Contact and Threats**

**E'Lise's Claim**: 

Alleges ongoing harassment, threats to her children, and detailed knowledge of Mr. Nuno's intentions.

**Documented Reality**:

- Judge Warren asked E'Lise **twice** under oath whether Mr. Nuno had made any contact beyond the three 2017 emails - she answered "NO" both times
- No evidence of threats has ever been produced or documented in any court proceeding
- E'Lise admitted the only communication was three emails requesting cessation of harassment in direct contradiction to her written statements.

**Legal Significance**: 

The contradiction between her sworn testimony and her written statements constitutes perjury under Montana law MCA 45-7-202.

## 4. **False Claims About "Stalking" and Contact Attempts**

**E'Lise's Claim**: 

Describes elaborate stalking behavior and rambling texts.

**Documented Reality**:

- All documented contact was either emergency-related or harassment cessation requests
- Court records show Mr. Nuno and Danielle were in regular bi-directional contact until October 2015, with Danielle regularly accepting financial support from Mr Nuno through until December 2015, contradicting stalking narrative
- No evidence of "rambling texts" has been produced in any court proceeding
- Judge Warren determined Mr. Nuno's conduct constituted protected First Amendment speech, finding no evidence of threatening communication or in-person contact

## 5. **Fabricated Claims About Legal Proceedings**

**E'Lise's Claim**: 

States Mr. Nuno "left Seattle without completing his sentencing."

**Documented Reality**: 

Mr. Nuno fled Washington due to fear for his safety after multiple near-fatal police encounters, not to avoid legal obligations. Additionally Officer Ethan Smith, which she heavily relies on in her written statement, was forcibly removed from the case due to "obvious appearance of impropriety,” and a formal complaint filed against him by Mr Nuno’s parents demanding he cease and desist all attempts to contact them, refrain from repeatedly confronting them, and cease conducting surveillance of their homes.

E'Lise's statements meet all elements of criminal defamation under MCA 45-8-212:

1. **Knowingly communicated** false statements to law enforcement
2. **Defamatory matter** that injured Mr. Nuno's reputation and occupation
