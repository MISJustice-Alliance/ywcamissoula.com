## Complaint Summary

- **Tyrone Nuno filed a complaint against Officer Ethan Smith (Badge #345) in March 2018**
- **Officer Smith repeatedly contacted Tyrone Nuno and his ex-wife Ellie Nuno regarding their son Elvis Nuno starting June 2017**
- **Contact attempts included visiting Ellie's residence and numerous phone calls, messages, and letters over several months**
- **Tyrone cooperated initially with one brief conversation but officer continued harassment despite having no new information**
- **Complainant alleges unprofessional conduct - contacting family members about matters they're uninvolved in**
- **Tyrone believes officer is fishing for information without legitimate cause and attempting to intimidate his son**
- **Formal request made for all contact attempts with family to cease immediately**

Following this complaint and subsequent conversations between Mr. Nuno's father and Sergeant Delridge, Ethan Smith was removed from any case involving Mr. Nuno and E'Lise Chard. The removal was due to the appearance of impropriety and unbecoming conduct stemming from his harassment of Mr. Nuno's elderly parents.

### Letter Text:

This complaint is regarding officer Ethan Smith (Badge #345). Beginning in June of 2017 officer Smith showed up at my ex wife's (Ellie Nuno) residence at [REDACTED] asking questions about my son Elvis Nuno. Since that time he has made at least a half dozen other attempts to contact me and my ex wife. He has incessantly harassed myself, Tyrone Nuno, and ex wife, Ellie Nuno, with phone calls, messages, and letters. At first I was cooperative with him and I spoke with him briefly once which was fine. But since then he has continued calling me and leaving messages for me and my ex wife. This is highly unprofessional and unethical conduct for a police officer to confront someone's family members over a matter they have nothing to do with. I would like to be left alone seeing as how I don't know anything, and it causes a great deal of tension in my family. It seems to me like he is greatly exaggerating whatever this situation is and has no reason to believe my son is actually doing anything wrong, but is simply fishing for information and is trying to embarrass, humiliate, and intimidate my son. I do not want to be part of this, and am incredibly angered by this officer's unbecoming behavior and incessant harassment. Please, I am asking that I be left alone, and that all attempts to contact myself and my family members cease and desist immediately.

Thank you very much for your time and consideration.
